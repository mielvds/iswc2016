Registration

The registration desk is located on the 5th floor of the Kobe international conference center. And it will be open the following:

  • Monday and Tuesday, 17 and 18 October: 8:00am - 6:00pm
  • Wednesday, 19 October: 8:00am - 9:30pm
  • Thursday, 20 October: 8:00am - 6:00pm
  • Friday, 21 October: 8:00am - 3:30pm

Lunch

Lunch will be held in Ariston Hotel, which is a 3 minutes walk from the entrance of the venue. See below for the floors on each day.

  • Monday and Tuesday, 17 and 18 October: the 2nd and 5th floor
  • Wednesday, 19 October: the 2nd, 5th and 16th floor
  • Thursday, 20 October: the 2nd and 5th floor. Mentoring Lunch will be held on the 16th floor
  • Friday, 21 October: the 2nd, 5th and 16th floor.

Vegitarian and Halal meals are available on the 5th floor. Extra lunch ticket (2,000JPY/day) is offered at the registration desk.
